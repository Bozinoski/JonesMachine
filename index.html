Use the higlihted knobs to jog each axis to near the middle of its travel range.


The knob higlighted in green controls the speed, where .0001 is slowest and .01 is fastest.


Rotating the dial (highlighted in blue) counter-clockwise will bring the wheel (Y axis) down, the table (X axis) to the left, and also the table (Z axis) closer to you.


Switch between axis by moving the knob highlighted in red.
